Frequently Asked Questions

Tenant representation means you have a licensed commercial real estate broker working exclusively on your behalf to find space. We identify options that match your criteria, arrange tours, analyze lease terms, and negotiate the best deal possible. Unlike going directly to a landlord's agent, your tenant rep has a fiduciary duty to protect your interests.
In commercial real estate, the landlord typically pays the full commission which is then split between the listing agent and the tenant's agent. This means you receive professional representation at no additional cost. The commission is already built into the deal whether you use a tenant rep or not — so there is no reason not to have an expert in your corner.
It depends on your timeline and requirements. For straightforward office or retail searches, we can typically identify and tour viable options within 1-2 weeks. Lease negotiations usually take an additional 2-4 weeks. Complex requirements like specialized industrial facilities or build-to-suit projects may take longer. We work on your schedule and adapt to your urgency.
We cover all of the Wasatch Front and beyond — Salt Lake County, Utah County, Davis County, Weber County, and surrounding areas. Our team has deep relationships with landlords and property managers throughout the state, giving us access to both listed and off-market opportunities you will not find on your own.
