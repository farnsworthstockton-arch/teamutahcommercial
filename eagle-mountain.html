Property Overview

Monte Vista Ranch is a 482-acre land assemblage in Eagle Mountain, Utah — offered as a portfolio or by individual parcel. The property spans commercial, industrial, and agricultural zoning along one of the Wasatch Front's most rapidly developing corridors.

Eagle Mountain has emerged as a strategic node for data center development, light industrial, and master-planned commercial. The site benefits from proximity to the Sweetwater Industrial overlay and the Regional Technology Industrial (RTI) corridor, with infrastructure either in place or planned along Pony Express Parkway, Lake Mountain Road, and Piper Lane.

Individual parcels range from 0.84-acre infill industrial lots up to 116 acres of corridor commercial — providing flexibility for end-users, developers, and land bankers. Available parcels are listed below with current pricing.
