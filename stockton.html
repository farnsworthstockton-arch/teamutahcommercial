About

Stockton Farnsworth has been a licensed Utah real estate professional since July 2015. After completing the advanced broker curriculum and qualifying experience required by the Utah Division of Real Estate, Stockton upgraded his license to an active Utah Real Estate Associate Broker License — earned through the Division's 120-hour broker education program, passage of the broker examination, and documented full-time licensed real estate experience. As an Associate Broker with Team Utah Commercial at RE/MAX Associates, Stockton specializes in industrial, retail, office, land, multi-family, and hospitality transactions across Utah.

Committed to continuous education, Stockton actively pursues advanced certifications across negotiation, listing strategy, business development, commercial sales & leasing, marketing, and emerging technology — bringing modern, data-driven expertise to every client engagement.
